DrawLoop Instructions
How to make a loopy drawing:
  • Click a draw tool in the "toolbox" section
  • Draw something on the canvas!
  • When you release the mouse, a module will pop into the "tools" section. You can modify or delete your sound drawing here.
  • If you like what you made, hit "save" in top right corner to save to the gallery.
  • Press "new" to start over
  • Press "gallery" to see a gallery of other loopings
